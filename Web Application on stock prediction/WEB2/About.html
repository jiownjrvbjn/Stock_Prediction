What is Indexs and How are we using it to make a prediction for inflation and recession

An index is a method to track the performance of a group of assets in a standardized way. Indexes typically measure the performance of a basket of securities intended to replicate a certain area of the market. So basically we are using top indexes of a coutry like Nifty or Nasdaq 100 which have the leading stocks of that particular economy and represent the size and the well being of the economy which we use to take out the inlfation rates and calculate the recession chances.
